üìù How to Report a Bug

1
Email Our Team
Send a detailed email to bugs@jnappstore.com
2
Describe the Issue
Clearly explain what happened and what you expected to happen
3
Provide Steps to Reproduce
List exact steps to reproduce the bug so we can verify it
4
Attach Evidence
Include screenshots, error messages, or video recordings
5
System Information
Mention your device, browser version, and operating system
6
We Respond
Our team will investigate and get back to you within 2-3 business days

‚ö†Ô∏è What We Can't Help With

Account Issues: For login, password reset, or account-related problems, please contact support@jnappstore.com

App-Specific Issues: If a third-party app is crashing or misbehaving, contact the app developer instead

Feature Requests: These aren't bugs. Send feature requests to suggestions@jnappstore.com

Malware Reports: Found a malicious app? Use the "Report" button on the app page instead

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to fix a bug?
A: It depends on severity. Critical issues are fixed within hours. Major bugs within 1-2 days. Minor issues within 1-2 weeks.
Q: Will I be notified when my bug is fixed?
A: Yes! We'll send you an email notification when your reported bug is fixed in a new release.
Q: Can I track my bug report?
A: We'll provide a reference number via email. Include this when you need to check status.
Q: Should I post bugs on social media?
A: Please report through this system for faster resolution. Social media posts may be delayed or missed.
Q: What if I'm not sure if it's a bug?
A: Go ahead and report it anyway! Our team will determine if it's a bug or expected behavior.
Q: Can I suggest improvements while reporting?
A: Yes! Include improvement suggestions in your report. We value constructive feedback.
