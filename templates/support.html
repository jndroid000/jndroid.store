üåü Community Standards

To maintain a positive and respectful community, all members must follow these standards:

  • ‚úÖ Be Respectful
    Treat all community members with courtesy and respect. Disagreements are okay, but personal attacks are not.
  • ‚úÖ No Spam or Harassment
    Don't post repetitive messages, unsolicited advertising, or harassing content. Respect others' time and privacy.
  • ‚úÖ Authentic Feedback
    Provide honest and constructive reviews. Help developers improve by giving detailed feedback.
  • ‚úÖ No Illegal Content
    Don't share or promote illegal content, malware, or anything that violates laws.
  • ‚úÖ Protect Privacy
    Never share personal information of others without consent. Report any privacy violations.
  • ‚úÖ Stay On Topic
    Keep discussions relevant to the app or category. Use appropriate channels for different topics.

Guidelines for uploading and maintaining apps on JnDroid Store:

DO's:
‚úì Provide Clear Descriptions

Write detailed app descriptions so users understand what your app does and its features.

‚úì Keep Apps Updated

Regularly update your apps with bug fixes, improvements, and new features. Maintain version history.

‚úì Respond to Reviews

Engage with user feedback. Address issues, thank reviewers, and ask for specific feedback on improvements.

‚úì Use Accurate Metadata

Ensure app title, category, size, and version information are accurate and up-to-date.

‚úì Transparent About Permissions

Clearly state what permissions your app requires and why. Be transparent about data usage.

DON'Ts:
‚úó No Misleading Information

Don't use screenshots or descriptions that misrepresent your app's functionality.

‚úó No Malware or Spyware

Your app must not contain malware, spyware, or any code that harms user devices or steals data.

‚úó No Excessive Ads

Ads should enhance user experience, not overwhelm it. Disclose ad practices clearly.

‚úó No Copyright Infringement

Don't use copyrighted content without proper permission or licensing.

‚úó No Hateful or Offensive Content

Apps must not contain content that promotes hate, discrimination, or violence.

üìã Content Policy

Prohibited Content:
  • Illegal Activities
    Apps or content related to illegal activities, including drug manufacturing, distribution, or use.
  • Hate Speech & Discrimination
    Content promoting hatred based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability.
  • Violence & Harm
    Content that encourages or glorifies violence, self-harm, or dangerous activities.
  • Sexual Content
    Explicit sexual content, non-consensual intimate images, or child sexual abuse material.
  • Fraud & Scams
    Content designed to defraud, deceive, or scam users.
  • Misinformation
    Deliberately false or misleading information that could cause harm.

üîí Security & Privacy

For Users:
  • Verify Before Installing
    Check app reviews, permissions, and developer information before installation. Download only from trusted sources.
  • Report Suspicious Apps
    If an app seems malicious or violates policies, report it immediately to our moderation team.
  • Keep Your Device Secure
    Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and keep your device updated.
  • Protect Your Data
    Never share sensitive information like passwords or payment details in reviews or messages.
For Developers:
  • Data Protection
    Protect user data with encryption and secure practices. Have a clear privacy policy.
  • Minimal Permissions
    Request only necessary permissions. Explain why each permission is needed.
  • Secure Updates
    Provide security updates promptly when vulnerabilities are discovered.
  • Transparency
    Be transparent about data collection, third-party services, and analytics usage.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does app review take?
A: Most apps are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Complex apps may take longer. We'll notify you via email when your app is approved or rejected.
Q: Can I appeal a rejected app?
A: Yes! If your app is rejected, we'll explain why. You can fix the issues and resubmit. Contact support if you believe the rejection was incorrect.
Q: How do I update my app?
A: Log in to your dashboard, go to "My Apps," select the app, and upload the new version. You can add version notes and release information.
Q: Is it free to upload apps?
A: Yes! JnDroid Store is free for developers. You can upload unlimited apps without any fees.
Q: How can I report a suspicious app?
A: Use the report button on the app page. Select "Malicious/Spyware" and provide details. Our moderation team investigates reports promptly.
Q: Why was my review deleted?
A: Reviews violating community standards are removed. This includes spam, hate speech, or off-topic content. Read our guidelines to ensure your reviews comply.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to your account settings and select "Delete Account." Note: This will remove all your data and apps cannot be recovered.
Q: Is my data safe on JnDroid Store?
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Read our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.
