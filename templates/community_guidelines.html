üë®‚Äçüíª Developer Expectations

Developers agree to maintain high standards when publishing on JN App Store:

Developer Responsibilities
Accurate Metadata

Ensure app title, description, screenshots, and version info are accurate and current.

Security

No malware, spyware, viruses, or code designed to harm devices or steal data.

Privacy Compliance

Have a clear privacy policy. Be transparent about data collection and usage.

Respond to Users

Address user concerns and feedback professionally and promptly.

Regular Updates

Fix bugs, release security patches, and maintain version history.

Developer Prohibitions
No Misleading Apps

Screenshots, descriptions, or functionality must match what users actually get.

No Excessive Ads

Ad density should enhance, not diminish, user experience.

No Unauthorized Permissions

Request only necessary permissions. Explain why each is needed.

No Illegal Content

Apps promoting illegal activities will be removed.

‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement & Consequences

Levels of Enforcement
Level 1: Warning

First minor violation - user/developer receives a warning

Level 2: Suspension (24-72 hours)

Repeated violations or moderate offenses result in temporary suspension

Level 3: Extended Suspension (1-30 days)

Serious violations lead to extended account suspension

Level 4: Permanent Ban

Severe or repeated violations result in permanent removal

What Gets Enforced?
  • Spam & Manipulation: Fake reviews, vote manipulation, repetitive posts
  • Harassment: Targeting, threats, hate speech
  • Malware: Infected apps, viruses, spyware
  • Privacy Violations: Sharing personal information, unauthorized data collection
  • Fraud: Scams, deceptive practices
  • Policy Violations: Any breach of these guidelines
